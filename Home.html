Cloud Computing

What exactly is it?

Cloud computing is a way of storing information on a remote server to save storage on your devices (laptops, phones etc).

Every time you upload a file to a cloud service such as Google Drive, you are storing your data on a remote server.

Many people use this for making backups, transfering files between devices, or even just to save space and organize things.




Examples of Cloud Computing Services:

Google Drive

ICloud

Mega

Mediafire

Dropbox
